About Arawa Mixed Farm Ltd

Our Background

This company Name ‘Arawa Mixed Farm’ is picked from the name of Arawa Mountains located in Leanguru village, Fuda Parish Pakelle Sub County. Arawa to the eyes of directors is Magic Mountain with plentiful bamboo tree, very fertile neighborhood and the foot of the mountain is blessed with ever green belt and fresh stream that do not go dry even during the heart of dry season. In addition, Arawa Mountain is conspicuous mountain and it symbolizes the heritage of our forefathers. The name Arawa therefore bears clear Historical connotation and clearly associated with “Good Luck” Arawa mixed Farm Ltd started in July 2012 with fencing two (2) hectares at Anyuogweku family land for goat Farming. We introduce about 60 goats all local breed as starter seed. Later on we expanded and fenced another four (4) hectares to make the total number of fenced hectors to six (6), all for goats. Late in 2014, we acquired about 60 hectors of arable land at Ayirii. This land was purposely acquired for tree planting and seedlings production. To this date, we have planted and maintained close to 10 hectares of teak trees and eucalyptus at this locaion. In 2015 after realizing that the goat project has not picked as expected, we planned to introduce poultry section. Consequently, in August of the same year (2015) we started building 30x8 meter poultry home. This house got completed and birds introduced in 2016. In the same year of 2015, we acquired another 200 hectors of Farm land located at the foot of Arawa Mountains for cattle farming. The animal at this located where introduced on March 2016. Originally we introduced only 16 cattle local breed. But right now we have over 120 cows of mixed breed at this same location, all belonging to Arawa Mixed Farm Ltd. Around June 2016, we acquired another 80 hectares of farm land at Nyambila. This land is speciﬁcally acquired for crop production and seed multiplication. To this day, very active crop production and homeland seed multiplication business is taking place at this location. Early last year, we planned and build sizable house for piggery section. Total of 7 pigs of local breed were introduced. Today, we have over 50 pigs of diﬀerent breeds. We for example have comborough, large white, land race etc Again in 2018, we acquired another 200 hectare of farm land at Carakwe near indriani in Okawa village Lewa Parish for second cattle farming location. This place is now home of best cattle breeds in the district and it is undergoing serious further developments to render it safe to received more sensitive cattle breed like Friesian, Guernsey and jersey cattle breeds.
